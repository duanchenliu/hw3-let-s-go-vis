Finally, a visualization!

Map: Zoom in and out, and drag the map to find the country you want to see more about. Hover over a country to see its name. Click on a country's capital to see its correspondent “World Happiness Report” data on the scatterplot!

Scatter plot: The size of the bubbles represents population. Choose the X-Axis and Y-Axis to see correlation between variables like happiness rank, GDP per Capita, Family-friendly, Freedom, and Generosity. Brush an area on the scatter plot → zoom in. Double click on the scatter plot → zoom out.
